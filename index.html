ENOUGH

An Interactive Picture Book

Quick Info

  • Author: Isaac Cohen ( 'Cabbibo' )
  • Runtime: 20 - 40 minutes
  • Release Date: 6 / 18 / 2015
  • Release Location: http://cabbi.bo/enough
  • Price: Free
  • Required Technology: Google Chrome, Modern GPU, Laptop / Desktop
  • Contact: isaaclandoncohen@gmail.com

Summary

'Enough' is story of a small creature named Mani trying to find its way in the world. Mani wakes up in an unknown location, and explores the world around it, page by page. As Mani discovers new lands Mani begins to wonder why it is alive, and tries to find its truth. Each new page contains new sites and sounds, as well as a unique instrument that the user can play.

As Mani learns more about the land it lives in, Mani goes through a learning process of trying to understand its purpose. Over the course of the 10 pages, Mani finds and loses friends, swims through a sequencer forest, enters a golden sphere, and discovers many other audio-visual delights. Each page has a song that is crafted for the specific visuals and words of that enviornment.
